Help Us Save Animals

Your donation can make a difference

Every year, thousands of animals are abandoned, abused, or left to fend for themselves. At Paws & Care Foundation, we rescue these vulnerable animals, provide medical care, and find them loving forever homes.

With your support, we can continue our vital work. Just $50 can feed a rescued animal for a month, $100 can cover vaccinations, and $500 can help with life-saving surgery.
